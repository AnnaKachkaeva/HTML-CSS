Sheriff Woody Pride, commonly known simply as Woody, is the main character in the Toy Story franchise created by Pixar. He is a pullstring cowboy rag doll and one of the two leaders (along with Buzz Lightyear) of the toys in the movies. His facial features are based on Tone Thyne, a former Disney animator. He is voiced by Tom Hanks and by Tom's brother Jim Hanks in video games and merchandise.
